Movie Ranking Project

The Best Anime Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Your Name

Directed by Makoto Shinkai, "Your Name" is a visually stunning film that tells the story of two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies. The movie beautifully explores themes of love, fate, and the connection between people.

A Silent Voice

"A Silent Voice" is a powerful film that addresses bullying, redemption, and the struggles of communication. It follows the journey of a former bully seeking forgiveness

Weathering with You

Another masterpiece by Makoto Shinkai, "Weathering with You" tells the story of a boy who runs away to Tokyo and meets a girl who can manipulate the weather. The film beautifully captures the themes of climate change, love, and hope.
